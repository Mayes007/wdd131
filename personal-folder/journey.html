Their Journey

Obi-Wan's Journey

Obi-Wan Kenobi’s journey begins as a young Padawan under the mentorship of Qui-Gon Jinn. After his master’s death, he takes on the responsibility of training Anakin Skywalker. Throughout the Clone Wars, Obi-Wan demonstrates his commitment to the Jedi Order and his unwavering belief in the greater good. His journey is marked by trials, loss, and ultimate redemption, as he continues to fight for peace and justice even in exile.

Anakin Skywalker's Journey

Anakin Skywalker’s journey is one of the most tragic in the Star Wars saga. Discovered as a slave with immense potential, Anakin rises to become one of the most powerful Jedi. However, his fear of loss and desire for power lead him down a dark path. His transformation into Darth Vader represents his fall, but ultimately, his love for his son Luke Skywalker brings about his redemption. Anakin's journey is a tale of ambition, fall, and redemption.
