Frindle by Andrew Clements

Genre: Realistic Fiction, Humor

Summary: Nick Allen creates a new word—"frindle"—as a clever idea that turns into a national sensation.

What I liked: It's fun, creative, and makes you think about how language works. Great for classroom discussions too!

Recommended Age: 8–11 years old
