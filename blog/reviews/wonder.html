Wonder by R.J. Palacio

Genre: Realistic Fiction, Drama

Summary: August Pullman, a boy with a facial difference, attends school for the first time and faces challenges and kindness from classmates.

What I liked: This book is emotional, powerful, and full of heart. It teaches compassion and the importance of choosing kindness.

Recommended Age: 10+ years old
